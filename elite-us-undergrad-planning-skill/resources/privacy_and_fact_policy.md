# Privacy and Fact Policy

- Do not use one student's private details in another student's report.
- Keep reusable templates anonymous.
- Do not invent awards, rankings, scores, or official paths.
- Distinguish `事实`, `学生/家长陈述`, `顾问判断`, and `推断`.
- When a fact is uncertain, write `需核验`.
- When a required fact is missing, write `信息待补齐`.
- Never upgrade an award level unless there is a certificate or official result.
- For sensitive family budget/aid information, avoid unnecessary detail in general summaries.
